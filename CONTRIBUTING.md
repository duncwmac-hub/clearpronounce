# Contributing to ClearPronounce

This repository is for **ClearPronounce business assets only**. Personal notes and life-organisation data live in `~/DuncanOS` and should not be added here.

## What belongs in this repo

- Product and curriculum content (e.g. Unit Zero materials, pronunciation guides, worksheets).
- Client-facing documents (school explainers, pilot proposals, pitch decks, brochures).
- Code and automation that directly support ClearPronounce as a product (e.g. generation scripts, website code).

## What does *not* belong here

- Personal GTD lists, capture/inbox items, or session logs.
- Personal finances, health notes, or other non-business context.
- Raw thinking and emotional processing about ClearPronounce (those stay in `~/DuncanOS/projects/` and `~/DuncanOS/capture/`).

## Relationship to DuncanOS

- **DuncanOS (`~/DuncanOS`)** is your personal operating system: projects, routines, decisions, and private notes.
- **ClearPronounce repo (`~/clearpronounce`)** is the shareable business repository: everything in here should be safe to show to collaborators, schools, or investors.

When an idea or document in DuncanOS becomes a real ClearPronounce artefact (e.g. a school explainer, pilot pack, or template), create or move it into this repo and update the asset index in `~/DuncanOS/projects/clearpronounce_assets.md` to point here.
